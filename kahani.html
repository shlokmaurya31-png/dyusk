The Story

कहानी

Every brand has an origin story. Ours starts on a gym floor in India, with a shirt that couldn’t keep up.

01 — The Itch

A shirt that quit before we did.

DYUSK began as a complaint. Compression wear sold in India was either imported at absurd prices or built to look technical without doing anything technical. Fabric that bagged out by the third wash. Seams that mapped to nothing. Pressure that vanished the moment you started sweating. We kept buying it, and it kept quitting before the session did.

02 — The Name

The hour between done and dark.

DYUSK comes from dusk — the hour the gym empties out. The office crowd leaves, the music gets worse, and the people who stay are the ones doing the unglamorous work. That hour is our customer. The K is for kaam — the work itself.

03 — The Standard

One pressure standard, no decoration.

We spent a year on a single question: where does the body actually need pressure under load? The answer became Compression System 01 — two silhouettes, six graded zones, and a rule we refuse to break: if a panel doesn’t have a job, it doesn’t ship.

04 — What’s Next

Compression System 02 is in the lab.

New colourways, a bottoms programme, and a recovery layer are all in wear-testing right now — on real lifters, not mannequins. The list hears about it first; the homepage hears about it second.

The journey, one pressure at a time.
