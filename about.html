About Us

Built by a lifter.

DYUSK is founder-led, India-made, and unreasonable about one thing: pressure that stays where it’s put.

“I didn’t start a clothing brand. I started a standard, and it happens to be wearable.”

Shlok Maurya — Founder, DYUSK

01 — Why We Exist

Imported prices. Decorative pressure. No thanks.

Serious training gear in India meant paying import margins for products engineered around somebody else’s climate and somebody else’s body. DYUSK exists to build world-standard compression here — priced for the people who actually train in it.

02 — What We Make

Two silhouettes. One job.

A Full Sleeve and a Half Sleeve, each in three colourways, each graded across six seam zones. We would rather do two products at an obsessive standard than twenty at an acceptable one.

03 — How We Work

Small batches, tested on the floor.

Every run is wear-tested through real training blocks before it ships — cold starts, long sessions, bad washing habits included. If a batch fails the floor test, it never sees the website.

Made to be worn hard.

Washed. Repeated. Outworked.
