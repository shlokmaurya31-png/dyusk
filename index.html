02 — Pressure Map

Every zone earns its compression.

Nothing on a DYUSK garment is decorative. Each panel is graded for a specific job the body does under load.

  • 01

    Shoulder Drive

    Directional stretch panelling that moves with rotation instead of resisting it — for pressing, throwing, and overhead work.

  • 02

    Core Stability

    Graduated mid-density compression through the torso, tuned to support posture without restricting the diaphragm.

  • 03

    Lat Compression

    Higher-density side panels reduce muscle oscillation on lateral movement and change of direction.

  • 04

    Cuff Lock

    Silicone-free grip binding keeps the sleeve or hem in place through a full session — no mid-set adjustment.
