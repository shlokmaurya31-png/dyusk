Artisans

DYUSK's patterns draw on centuries of craft from around the world. These are the traditions our ideas come from.

Asia

  • Intricate geometric and nature-inspired paintings using vibrant natural colors.

  • Minimalist white figures depicting everyday village life.

  • Detailed mythological narratives with decorative borders.

  • Famous for landscapes, actors, and scenes of daily life.

  • Elegant monochrome paintings emphasizing simplicity.

  • Expressive brushwork capturing landscapes and philosophy.

  • Blue, turquoise, and red floral tile designs.

Africa

  • Bold geometric murals painted on homes.

  • Colorful woven cloth with symbolic patterns.

  • Regal beadwork used in ceremonial objects.

  • Intricate jewelry and symbolic ornaments.

Europe

  • Flowing floral motifs painted on wood.

  • Gold, red, and black floral patterns.

  • Endless interwoven patterns representing eternity.

  • Gold-glass mosaics found in churches.

Americas

  • Brightly painted fantastical animal sculptures.

  • Sacred geometric compositions made with yarn and beads.

  • Geometric blankets and rugs.

  • Layered appliqué textiles with colorful motifs.

Oceania

  • Symbolic dot patterns telling Dreamtime stories.

  • Spirals and ancestral motifs carved into wood and stone.

  • Decorative cloth made from tree bark.

Middle East & North Africa

  • Complex repeating geometric and star patterns.

  • Richly detailed illustrations with vibrant colors.

  • Hand-cut geometric tile patterns.
