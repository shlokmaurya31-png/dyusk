Philanthropy

We call it Ground Up: one percent of every order goes to grassroots Indian athletes — as gear, not press releases.

01 — The Problem

Talent is everywhere. Kit is not.

India does not lack athletes. It lacks basics at the bottom of the pyramid — shoes that fit, kit that survives a season, recovery that isn’t a luxury. Sponsors arrive after the podium. Nobody funds the years before it.

02 — The Programme

One percent, every order, automatically.

Ground Up takes one percent of every DYUSK order — not profit, revenue — and converts it into equipment grants for district-level athletes and their coaches. No applications written in English required. Coaches nominate; we deliver kit.

03 — The Receipts

We publish what we give.

Every quarter we publish the number: orders taken, amount pooled, kit delivered, and to whom (with their permission). If the number is small, we publish it small. Honesty scales better than marketing.

04 — Get Involved

Know a team that needs kit?

If you coach or train with a grassroots squad that needs equipment, write to us — the contact page reaches a human. Nominations from the floor beat any spreadsheet we could build.
